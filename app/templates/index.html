Rock Paper Scissors

Welcome to Rock Paper Scissors. To play, enter your name on the registration page and choose which version of the game that you would like to play. You have the choice of the classic gesture game where you choose from the three eponymous hand gestures, each beatable by one of the others, and capable of victory over the other. The other choice is Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock. This is an expanded version of the game in which the additional two options mean that each gesture will beat two of the others and in turn are beaten by the remaining two. The advantage of this version is that it results in fewer draws.

Finally, choose whether you will compete against another human or the computer.
